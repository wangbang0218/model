How it works

Free plan available

Choose a trigger

How would you like to start your workflow?

  • When something happens in one of your apps.
  • When a form on your website is submitted.

Add some tasks

Then choose what you want to do in your apps:

  • Send an email.
  • Add a new customer.
  • Get an invoice, etc.

Each task can use data you collect in previous tasks.

Use filters to add logic

Filters allow you to decide whether to run a task.

You could use filters to send different messages depending on what type of product a customer bought.

Wait for a while

Optionally, you can wait for hours, days, weeks or even years to pass between tasks.

Great for marketing automation or reminders.
